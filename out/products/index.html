What We’re Building

In active research & development

Our first machine is taking shape. We’re holding model names and specifications back while the work matures — here is the direction it’s heading.

Status: R&D
The concept

One machine that extrudes and converts a woven bag by itself

Built on Industry 5.0 architecture, the system is designed to take polymer in and produce a finished woven bag out — handling the entire cycle within a single closed unit. It is the first product of a company set up to rethink how technical textiles are made.

Integrated

Extrusion through conversion, unified in one machine — no relay between separate stations.

Autonomous

The system runs the full cycle on its own, coordinating each stage without manual hand-offs.

Closed architecture

A single self-contained unit engineered as a whole, not assembled from off-the-shelf parts.

Where it stands

From architecture to a working machine

The path is staged and deliberate. We’re proving each subsystem before bringing them together under one control layer.
